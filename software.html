Software marketing is the process of promoting software products or services to attract and retain customers. It involves strategies like content marketing, paid campaigns, SEO, email marketing, and user engagement to drive awareness, leads, and conversions.

Software companies generate leads through a mix of inbound marketing (blogs, SEO, webinars, gated content), outbound campaigns (cold outreach, targeted ads), and nurturing strategies that convert prospects into paying customers.
